Highlights

  • The National Level Science Talent Search Examination

    The National Level Science Talent Search Examination, NSTSE 2021 was conducted by the Unified Council India on 24rth and 31st january 2021.Students pf Dps Indirapuram did the school proud by winning laurels in this prestigious examination. Raghav Agarwal class X A secured rank 16 among the top 100 National Achievers . Rohan Saini of class IX A secured rank 73 among the top 100 National achievers of class IX. At the State Level, Tushay Gupta of class XA secured rank 2 across Uttar Pradesh among class X achievers. Saksham Pratap Singh of Class X B attained rank 3 across Uttar Pradesh among class X achievers.



  • 82nd Junior and Youth National Table Tennis Championships 2020-21

    Radhapriya Goel, a student of Class XIl of Dps indirapuram did the school proud by winning the bronze medal in the Youth Girls Singles (under 21years) event at the 82nd Junior and Youth National Table Tennis Championships 2020-21 held at Abhay Prashal Stadium, Indore, Madhya Pradesh from 7th to 16th March. Radhapriya secured the 3rd place in the top 4 among 125 players who participated in this tournament from all across the country. Radhapriya's achievement is a testimony to her grit, determination and sportsmanship spirit. Congratulations Radhapriya on your incredible feat!
